# Project Brief: AI-Driven Recruitment & Talent Ecosystem

## Executive Summary
This project aims to build a **production-ready, multi-tenant SaaS Recruitment & Talent Ecosystem** that transcends traditional job boards. It is a comprehensive platform integrating social networking (LinkedIn-style), AI-driven recruitment automation, and a robust B2B SaaS model for enterprises.

The system is designed with a **"Startup-Grade" mindset**: scalable to millions of users, architected for microservices evolution, and optimized for monetization and global expansion. It serves as a unified ecosystem for Candidates, Recruiters (HR), Employers, and Administrators, bridging the gap between talent acquisition and professional networking.

## Core Vision
1.  **Ecosystem, Not Just a Board:** A holistic platform combining social engagement, job matching, and talent management.
2.  **AI as a Core Capability:** Deep integration of AI for candidate scoring, chatbots, and automated workflows, not just a gimmick.
3.  **SaaS B2B First:** Built from the ground up with multi-tenancy, subscription tiers, and enterprise-grade security.
4.  **Mobile-First & Offline-Ready:** distinct React Native mobile app with offline synchronization for unified user experience.
5.  **Investment-Ready Architecture:** Clean Architecture, DDD, and Event-Driven design to demonstrate technical maturity and scalability to investors.

## Strategic Goals
-   **Scalability:** Support 1M+ concurrent users via horizontal scaling, Redis caching, and Kafka event streaming.
-   **Monetization:** Flexible subscription models (Free, Basic, Pro, Enterprise) and consumption-based billing (Boosts, AI usage).
-   **Efficiency:** Reduce time-to-hire by 40% using AI matching and automated interview scheduling.
-   **Global Reach:** Multi-language, multi-currency support, and GDPR-compliant data handling.

## Scope
### In-Scope
-   **Multi-tenant SaaS Core:** Tenant isolation, Subscription management, Role-based access control (RBAC).
-   **Recruitment Engine:** Job posting, Application tracking (ATS), Interview scheduling.
-   **Social Platform:** User feeds, Networking, Company updates.
-   **AI Integration:** Candidate scoring, Resume parsing, Chatbot assistants.
-   **Mobile App:** React Native app for Candidates and HR with offline scheduling and push notifications.
-   **Payment & Billing:** Integration with payment gateways, tiered subscription logic.
-   **Analytics:** Job performance, hiring funnels, tenant usage metrics.

### Out-of-Scope (for MVP)
-   Video conferencing hosting (Integration with Zoom/Google Meet only).
-   Payroll management system.
-   Complex VR/AR onboarding experiences.

## Success Metrics (KPIs)
-   **System Uptime:** 99.9%.
-   **API Latency:** < 300ms (95th percentile).
-   **AI Match Accuracy:** > 85% relevance feedback.
-   **User Retention:** > 40% Month-over-Month.
